Get to Know Me !

My name is Ayrionna Christian, I am 20 years old. I am a current graphic designer student at Central Michigan University. I have a passion for visual storytelling and creativity. Currently, I'm pursuing my degree in Graphic design planning to receive my BFA. I am constantly building my skills in design software, typography and digital media. I enjoy creating different designs and logos that are both visually engaging and functional. I'm always eager to push my creativity and finding my inner artist.

Biggest Inspiration

Erron Chandler, 28 year old artist, whom is my God Brother, inspired me to dive into the artistic field. His journey reminded me of mines when I first started. He stated, “I spent a lot of time with colored pencils. Being very bored at work opened my imagination, which prepared me for the next step.I got a paint set for secret Santa at work and that started my painting career. When I started painting, I came up with the name “Fill Your Void” because that what it felt like what I was doing for myself; filling in that negative space with creativity.”-Erron Chandler
