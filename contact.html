My Journey

I began my artistic journey during the Covid-19 lockdown, when I found myself confined to a room with nothing but time and a restless mind. To break the monotony, I picked up a notebook and started sketching—just simple lines at first, but soon it became a daily habit. That creative spark led me to experiment with painting on canvas, exploring color, texture, and expression. Over time, my passion evolved, and I transitioned into the world of digital design, where I continue to grow and create today. Art and design became more than just a way to pass the time—it turned into a passion and a powerful form of self-expression. What started as a creative escape during isolation has grown into a meaningful journey, shaping both my skills and identity as an artist.
